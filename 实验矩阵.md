## 核心实验矩阵（2×3消融设计）

|  | 基线AL (random/entropy/margin/coreset/badge/qbc) | 创新AL (class_aware/gap_aware/adaptive_gap) |
|---|---|---|
| **无SSL** | `std_al` ✅ | `innovative_al` ⏳ |
| **基础SSL** (FlexMatch) | `al_ssl` ✅ | `innovative_al_ssl_basic` ❌ 补齐 |
| **创新SSL** (deficit阈值+类别加权) | `al_ssl_innovative` ❌ 补齐 | `innovative_al_ssl` ✅ |

**消融逻辑**:
- 对角线: std_al → innovative_al_ssl (从基线到联合创新)
- 隔离AL创新: al_ssl vs innovative_al_ssl_basic (固定基础SSL，换AL策略)
- 隔离SSL创新: al_ssl vs al_ssl_innovative (固定基础AL，换SSL方法)
- 联合效应: innovative_al_ssl vs (innovative_al_ssl_basic + al_ssl_innovative) (联合 > 单独之和?)

**脚本**:
- `run_innovative_al_basic_ssl.py` → innovative_al_ssl_basic
- `run_al_ssl_innovative.py` → al_ssl_innovative
